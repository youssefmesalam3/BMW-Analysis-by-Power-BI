# 🚗 BMW Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The BMW Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze BMW vehicle sales performance across different models, regions, fuel types, transmission types, and vehicle colors.

The dashboard provides comprehensive business insights into revenue generation, sales volume, product performance, and market distribution, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions and identify growth opportunities. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall BMW sales performance.
- Analyze revenue and unit sales across vehicle models.
- Evaluate regional sales distribution.
- Compare fuel type preferences.
- Analyze automatic versus manual transmission performance.
- Identify top-performing vehicle colors and models.
- Support strategic sales and marketing decisions.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Regional Performance
- Asia generated the highest revenue among all regions.
- Europe and North America closely followed in total revenue.
- Revenue distribution remained relatively balanced across regions. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

### Model Performance
- BMW 7 Series generated the highest unit sales.
- i8, X1, and 3 Series were among the top-performing models.
- Sales volume remained competitive across multiple vehicle models. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

### Transmission Analysis
- Automatic and Manual transmissions showed nearly equal distribution.
- Automatic vehicles slightly outperformed manual vehicles in total sales volume. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

### Fuel Type Analysis
- Hybrid vehicles recorded the highest order count.
- Petrol and Electric vehicles showed similar demand levels.
- Diesel vehicles generated the lowest order count among fuel categories. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

### Color Analysis
- Red vehicles generated the highest revenue.
- Silver and White vehicles followed closely behind.
- Customer preferences were distributed across multiple color options. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
